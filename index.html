      For Grown-Ups

      How to help a 3rd grader learn gradually

      Keep it tiny

      • Do one lesson at a time
      • Let the child stop after one success
      • Repeat favorite projects with new themes
      • Do not rush into typing

      Ask helpful questions

      • What do you think will happen?
      • Which block starts the project?
      • What changed after you tested it?
      • What is one tiny thing to try next?
